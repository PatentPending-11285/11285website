Who are we?

We are a group of 9th - 12th grade students who love to build robots. We were established in 2016, and have competed in the FIRST® Tech Challenge (FTC) every year since. We like to try new designs every year - sometimes the gamble pays off, other times... not so much. We love doing what we do, and that's why we do it!

Club Stats

We established in 2016.

We currently have 15 students apart of the team: 7 seniors, 2 juniors, 3 sophomores, and 3 freshmen.

Our seniors plan to major in: Aerospace Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Computer Science.

We have competed in 11 FTC games, and in 2 Northrop Grumman High School Innovation Challenges.
