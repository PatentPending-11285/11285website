Build Process

Learn how Patent Pending Robotics designs, programs, and documents our robots.

Design

Patent Pending designs its robots in Onshape much like others. Our team’s CADers utilize the FTC Parts Library created by FTC Team 2901, Purple Gears. To gain access, email FIRST@ptc.com using your Onshape account email. Key components include a Limelight for AprilTag tracking, mecanum wheels for omnidirectional movement, and custom 3D printed and sheet metal parts.

Program

Our programmers develop both autonomous and driver-controlled systems. Autonomous (30 seconds) runs entirely on prewritten code using sensors. Driver control (2 minutes) uses gamepads connected to phones that send instructions to the robot. The final 30 seconds (End Game) focuses on bonus scoring objectives like parking.

Documentation

Documentation is a core part of FIRST. Our Engineering Notebook tracks the entire design process, decisions, and iterations. This helps us compete for awards like the Think Award and ensures our work is structured and intentional.

Outreach & Social Media

We use Instagram and community outreach to grow robotics interest. Our team participates in competitions, helps other teams, joins school events like club rush, and has participated in NIWC Pacific.
